Who We Are

Founded on Faith,
Community & Quality

Fresh & Green Halal Market was founded with one mission: to serve our Muslim and diverse local community with the highest quality halal food, rooted in authenticity, trust, and care.

Our journey began over a decade ago, born from a desire to create a welcoming space where finding authentic Halal products wasn't a challenge but a pleasure. We saw a need in Tukwila for a dedicated market that understood the cultural and religious significance of Halal at a deep level.

From humble beginnings we've grown alongside our community, expanding our offerings and building lasting relationships. Our commitment to strict Zabiha Halal standards from trusted suppliers is unwavering and our customers know it.
